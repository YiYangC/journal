title: "Museum as an Archive"
date: "2022-03-01"
excerpt: "How museums function as living archives — curating memory, constructing narrative, shaping culture."
tags: ["museum", "archive", "architecture"]
---

The museum is perhaps the most public form of archive — a space where the act of preservation becomes performative. Unlike personal collections hidden in drawers or digital files buried in folders, the museum archive demands an audience. It curates not just objects but the experience of encountering them.
