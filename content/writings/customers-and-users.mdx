title: "Customers & Users"
date: "2022-01-01"
excerpt: "The distinction between customers and users, and what it means for the things we design."
tags: ["technology", "design"]
---

The language we use to describe the people who interact with our products reveals our assumptions about the relationship. A customer implies a transaction. A user implies a tool. Neither captures the full complexity of the human on the other side of the screen.
